How to read this & caveats: ABS = confirmed in the listing, ABS* = assumed yes (2019+ model, where ABS is standard/typical) — still verify, No = model/year had no ABS, ? = not stated, verify with seller. ABS was often a separate model/option on these bikes (e.g. Honda "RA" = ABS), so a "?" doesn't mean it lacks ABS — check the VIN/bike in person. Condition ratings (★) are my rough read of each listing's wording, photos and detail level — not a physical inspection. Prices are asking prices; private sales usually exclude safety certification and the dealer/AS-IS listings exclude HST, licensing and OMVIC fees. Thumbnails are styled placeholders (marketplace image URLs are access-restricted); use "View ↗" for real photos. Modified flags insurance-relevant changes — aftermarket exhaust, lowering/suspension, engine/performance parts — as Yes; purely cosmetic items (tinted visor, plate tidy / fender eliminator, levers, tail tidy) count as No. Hover a badge for the specific part, and always disclose mods to your insurer. If a listing keeps the stock part (so the mod can be reverted), it is counted as No here. Travel times are rough one-way driving estimates from Oakville, ON in light traffic — verify your own route.
